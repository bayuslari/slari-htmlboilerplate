Meet Puri Asri

Hotel Puri Asri is strategically located at the centre of Magelang, Central Java. It is only 20 minutes from Borobudur Temple which was known world wide as one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It only takes 5 minutes drive to Borobudur International 18-Holes Golf Course.

The journey from Adi Sucipto International Airport Yogyakarta is just one hour ride.The atmosphere of the hotel is fascinating, surrounded with mountains and overlooking to river and huge greenery rice field with cool mountain breeze.
